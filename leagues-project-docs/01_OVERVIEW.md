## League Snapshot

- **League name:** Demonic Pacts (Leagues VI)
- **Launch date:** April 15, 2026
- **Key mechanic:** Pick one combat style (Magic / Melee / Ranged) at character creation — this is permanent. Earn 40 pact nodes by completing league tasks.
- **Resets:** 3 full pact tree resets, each earned by killing your first 3 echo bosses.

## Region Structure

- **Varlamore** — always unlocked (starting region), free
- **Karamja** — always unlocked (forced), free
- **3 regions chosen by the player** from 8 options: Kourend & Kebos, Fremennik, Morytania, Desert, Asgarnia, Kandarin, Tirannwn, Wilderness
- Total accessible regions: 5 (2 free + 3 chosen)
- Total possible 3-region combinations: 56

**Critical raid rule:** Twisted Bow, Scythe of Vitur, and Tumeken's Shadow drop from ALL THREE raids regardless of which single raid region is unlocked. You only need ONE raid region to access all mega-rare drops.

## Current High-Confidence Consensus

- **Magic is the dominant pact this league.** Thermonuclear Smoke Devil echo in Kandarin drops BiS magic mainhand + offhand weapons. No other pact has comparable weapon unlock.
- **Kandarin is near-mandatory for magic builds.** The Thermy echo weapons are the ceiling weapon for mage. Every serious magic build includes Kandarin.
- **Grimoire (T6 relic) is near-mandatory for magic builds.** Provides free spellbook swap, Ancient Magicks access without needing Desert, Augury and Rigour prayers without diary requirements. Replaces an entire region.
- **Kourend & Kebos is the strongest single region in the game.** Highest task density, CoX access, Alchemical Hydra.
- **Only one raid region is needed.** All mega-rares (Twisted Bow, Scythe, Shadow) drop from all raids regardless of which raid region you unlock.
- **Melee is called a "2H melee league"** — blindbag + ranged echo interactions on 2H weapons create unique builds. Not as universally dominant as magic but has unique mechanics.
- **Ranged is weaker than last league.** Crossbows can still miss even with the max-hit pact node. Community considers it a meaningful nerf vs Leagues 5.

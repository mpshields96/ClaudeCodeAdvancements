This file captures distilled intelligence from the Demonic Pacts Discord strategy channels (exported April 2026). 

**Do not treat this as hard facts.** These are community opinions, often pre-launch speculation. Where claims are also in the wiki/reference data (doc 02), confidence is higher.

## Repeated Community Archetypes

### 1. Magic Grimoire Mage (Most Popular — Pre-Launch)

- **Style:** Magic primary
- **Core regions:** Kandarin + Kourend + Morytania (or Desert as alt 3rd)
- **Core relics:** Endless Harvest or Abundance (T1), Friendly Forager (T2), Evil Eye (T3), Transmutation (T4), Soul Harvest (T5), **Grimoire (T6)**, Reloaded → Abundance (T7), Minion or Flask (T8)
- **Why it repeats:**
  - Thermy echo provides BiS magic weapons — no other region comes close
  - Grimoire eliminates need for Desert while giving Ancient Magicks + Augury free
  - Grotesque Guardians echo (Morytania) gives 4-tick earth/body staff — direct magic weapon
  - Kourend task density generates LP faster than any other region
- **Weaknesses:**
  - ToB is mechanically demanding — newer players may find Morytania harder to exploit
  - Earth spell interactions are more complex than simple casting
  - Some details about Thermy echo stats not fully revealed pre-launch

### 2. Melee Blindbag / 2H Build

- **Style:** Melee primary with ranged echo
- **Core regions:** Kourend + Morytania + Fremennik (or Desert)
- **Core relics:** Abundance (T1), Evil Eye (T3), Transmutation (T4), Culling Spree (T6)
- **Why it repeats:**
  - Blindbag mechanic creates unique gameplay not possible in normal OSRS
  - Ranged echoes on 2H melee weapons provide cross-style DPS
  - Community calls it a "2H melee league" — the new unique interaction
  - Recoil/thorns nodes synergise with high-defence tank builds
- **Community quote (high signal):** "Here's my melee Echo build! It combines some of the most powerful and interesting league mechanics: melee attack range, crossbow max hits, ranged echoes, and blindbag."
- **Weaknesses:**
  - Not as universally dominant as magic for most content types
  - Blindbag mechanics have several confirmed edge cases (Scythe triggers once per attack, not per hitsplat)

### 3. Ranged Crossbow Build

- **Style:** Ranged primary
- **Core regions:** Kourend + Fremennik + Tirannwn (or Kandarin)
- **Core relics:** Abundance (T1), Evil Eye (T3), Culling Spree (T6)
- **Why it repeats:**
  - Fremennik gives Vorkath (best early ranged boss) and DK echo helmet (BiS headslot)
  - Kourend gives Hespori echo shortbow (pre-Tbow ranged)
  - Still viable but widely acknowledged as a step down from Leagues 5 ranged
- **Community quote:** "Weakened vs last league — crossbows can miss even with max hit node. Last 2 leagues ranged was nuclear; this one feels different."
- **Weaknesses:**
  - Crossbows CAN still miss even with the max-hit pact node
  - Accuracy capstone addresses this but requires deeper tree investment
  - Community considers this the weakest of the three styles this league

### 4. Knives Out / Thrown Ranged

- **Style:** Ranged thrown weapons
- **Core regions:** Kourend + Wilderness + (3rd varies)
- **Core relics:** Abundance (T1), Evil Eye (T3), Culling Spree (T6)
- **Community quote:** "KNIVES OUT! Build. Attack style: Range. Minor melee effects as well."
- **Signal strength:** Moderate — interesting but niche; Wilderness is required and brings PvP risk.

### 5. Dad Route / Lazy Build

- **Style:** Magic or hybrid
- **Core regions:** Kandarin + Kourend + Morytania (same as top magic)
- **Core relics:** Endless Harvest (T1), Friendly Forager (T2), Evil Eye (T3), Transmutation (T4), Soul Harvest (T5), Grimoire (T6), Reloaded (T7), **Minion (T8)**
- **Community quote:** "No 2t sweating at all. No boring/long grinds/tasks. Max cape only because of the challenge, not grinding."
- **Why it repeats:**
  - AFK-playable while working or distracted
  - Minion T8 is universally applicable — no skill ceiling
  - Barrows (Morytania) gives good gear passively
  - Sulphur Nagua and Gem Crab in Varlamore are always-available AFK options
- **Weaknesses:**
  - Minion is lower DPS ceiling than Flask — harder endgame content takes longer
  - Endless Harvest has low PvM value if active bossing is also a goal

## Community-Wide Agreements

- **Magic will be meta.** Thermy echo is the strongest echo weapon combination announced. Universal agreement.
- **Kandarin = magic.** Not a choice, it's a requirement for any serious magic build.
- **Grimoire is near-mandatory for magic.** Replaces Desert, gives Augury, free spellbook swap. Most magic builds take it.
- **Evil Eye T3 is S-tier and almost always correct.** Teleports to any boss — eliminates all travel overhead.
- **Kourend is the best single region.** Highest task density, CoX raid, Alchemical Hydra. Wide agreement.
- **Only one raid region needed.** Mega-rares drop from all raids regardless of which raid region is unlocked.
- **Reloaded T7 is always valuable.** Best pick is usually the T1 you didn't take (Abundance if Endless Harvest; or Endless Harvest if Abundance).
- **Melee is a "2H melee league"** due to blindbag + ranged echo interactions. Unique this league.

## Community Splits

- **Morytania vs Desert as 3rd region for magic:**
  - **Morytania side:** Earth echo staff synergy with magic; ToB (Scythe); Barrows for mid-game; no region redundancy since Grimoire covers Ancient Magicks
  - **Desert side:** DT2 bosses (Soulreaper axe), GOTR RC task points, Nex, ToA (Fang)
  - **Verdict:** Morytania has more magic-specific synergy for a magic pact; Desert is better if you want DT2/GOTR points and don't mind the overlap with Grimoire

- **Endless Harvest vs Abundance for T1:**
  - **Endless Harvest:** Pure AFK — resources doubled, fishing spot follows, auto-bank. Best if work-AFK is primary playstyle.
  - **Abundance:** +10 skills, economy coins, unlocks Perilous Moons earlier (38 Slayer vs 48). Better for balanced PvM+skilling.
  - **Verdict:** Endless Harvest for pure AFK/skilling focus; Abundance for balanced PvM builds.

- **Minion vs Flask of Fervour for T8:**
  - **Minion side:** Universal, AFK-friendly, no skill ceiling, auto-loots, works everywhere. Best for casual/dad builds.
  - **Flask side:** Exceptional in raids; highest possible DPS output; but requires skill to time correctly. Zero value while AFK.
  - **Verdict:** Flask if you're doing end-game raids and skilled. Minion if you want passive utility with no learning curve.

- **Transmutation vs Butler's Bell for T4:**
  - Community shifted heavily toward Transmutation after April 8 reveal ("Transmutation is busted")
  - Butler's Bell still valid for pure AFK resource builds, but Transmutation's conversion utility is seen as more broadly valuable

## Lazy / Dad / AFK Recommendations

- Pick Endless Harvest T1 — resources doubled passively while doing anything.
- Friendly Forager T2 auto-handles herbs, a common bottleneck.
- Soul Harvest T5 converts combat loot to Prayer XP passively — great for any boss heavy build.
- Minion T8 is the universal AFK choice at endgame — Imp attacks automatically, loots automatically.
- Barbarian Fishing in Kandarin (AFK score 8) — playable while at work.
- Sulphur Nagua and Gem Crab in Varlamore are available from game start — always an AFK option.
- Barrows in Morytania: semi-AFK mid-game gear source.

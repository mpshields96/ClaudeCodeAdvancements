# Regions, Relics, and Tasks — Structured Reference

Facts in this file come from OSRS Wiki and official Jagex sources (as of April 8, 2026). Community opinions are in doc 03.

## Regions

### Varlamore (Always Unlocked — Starting Region)

- **Unlock:** Free, always accessible
- **Echo Boss:** Amoxliatl — drops combined 3-weapon item (difficulty: HARD)
- **Key content:** Perilous Moons (Eclipse/Blood/Blue Moon armor), Fortis Colosseum, Sulphur Nagua (best AFK melee XP 60+, passive RC XP), Gem Crab (AFK from game start), Cam Torum, Hunter Guild
- **AFK score:** 8/10 — excellent for passive play
- **Main reasons to pick:**
  - Starting region — automatically accessible
  - Sulphur Nagua: best AFK melee training, passive Runecrafting XP, playable while working
  - Gem Crab: AFK combat available from first login
  - Perilous Moons requires 48 Slayer (38 with Abundance relic)
- **Note:** Varlamore is an entirely new Leagues experience — no existing guides apply from prior leagues.

### Karamja (Forced Unlock)

- **Unlock:** Forced, always in every build
- **Echo Boss:** None assigned
- **Key content:** TzHaar / Fight Caves, Brimhaven Dungeon, Dragon imps
- **Note:** Zulrah is in Tirannwn (Zul-Andra), NOT Karamja (corrected April 8).
- **Main reasons to note:**
  - Universal starting region — plan around it, not for it
  - TzHaar solid for early combat progress
  - Fight Caves accessible to all builds early

### Kourend & Kebos

- **Unlock:** Choosable (3-region pick)
- **Raid:** Chambers of Xeric (CoX) — mega-rares drop from ALL raids, one raid region sufficient
- **Echo Boss:** Hespori — drops Root-forged shortbow (strong pre-Twisted Bow ranged weapon)
- **Key content:** CoX + Challenge Mode, Catacombs of Kourend, Skeletal Wyvern, Lizardman Shaman, Alchemical Hydra, Arceuus spellbook (via Grimoire if relic taken)
- **Task density:** VERY HIGH — highest of any region
- **PvM score:** 10/10
- **Main reasons to unlock:**
  - Highest task density in the game — best LP-per-time investment
  - Alchemical Hydra: strong mid-late game PvM
  - CoX access (but Twisted Bow / Shadow / Scythe also drop from ToB and ToA)
  - Catacombs: high bone density for Soul Harvest relic synergy
- **Magic synergy:** Arceuus spellbook available here; Grimoire makes this redundant

### Kandarin

- **Unlock:** Choosable
- **Echo Boss:** Thermonuclear Smoke Devil — drops **4-pronged magic mainhand + Devil's Element offhand** (EASY-MEDIUM difficulty)
- **Key content:** Thermy Echo (BiS magic weapons), Ranging Guild, Crystal areas, Barbarian Fishing (AFK, strong Agility/Strength XP), Essence mine
- **Task density:** HIGH
- **AFK score:** 7/10
- **Main reasons to unlock:**
  - **ESSENTIAL for magic builds** — Thermy echo = BiS magic weapon combination for the entire league
  - Barbarian Fishing: AFK score 8, playable while working, unlocks Agility/Strength XP passively
  - Crystal armour for ranged Pact builds
  - Essence mine (required for RC if Desert not taken)
- **Note:** Kandarin appears in every serious magic build recommendation without exception.

### Morytania

- **Unlock:** Choosable
- **Raid:** Theatre of Blood (ToB) — mega-rares drop from ALL raids
- **Echo Boss:** Grotesque Guardians — drops **4-tick earth/body staff** (MEDIUM difficulty; requires completing Slayer Tower roof section)
- **Key content:** ToB + HM ToB (all party members get unique drop in HM), Barrows, The Nightmare, Araxxor, Grotesque Guardians, Hallowed Sepulchre, Slayer Tower
- **Task density:** HIGH
- **PvM score:** 9/10
- **Main reasons to unlock:**
  - Grotesque Guardians echo = 4-tick earth/body staff (magic weapon — direct synergy with earth spells in Magic Pact)
  - ToB raid access (Scythe of Vitur)
  - Barrows: strong semi-AFK mid-game gear, accessible early
  - HM ToB: buffed drop rates, all party members get unique drop
- **Warning:** ToB is mechanically harder than CoX. Not recommended as first raid for players who haven't done it before.
- **Note:** Scythe of Vitur requires blood runes to charge, which are obtainable in this region.

### Desert

- **Unlock:** Choosable
- **Raid:** Tombs of Amascut (ToA) — includes Osmumten's Fang as exclusive mega-rare
- **Echo Boss:** Kalphite Queen — drops Drygore blowpipe (MEDIUM difficulty)
- **Key content:** ToA, ALL DT2 bosses with Desert unlock (drop Soulreaper axe pieces), Nex, GOTR (major RC task points), Ancient Magicks
- **Task density:** HIGH
- **PvM score:** 10/10
- **Main reasons to unlock:**
  - GOTR accessible with just Desert — huge source of RC task points
  - All DT2 bosses drop Soulreaper axe pieces with Desert unlock
  - ToA raid access; Osmumten's Fang exclusive to ToA
- **Magic synergy note:** Ancient Magicks available here, BUT Grimoire (T6 relic) makes this redundant — magic builds don't need Desert for spellbook access.

### Fremennik

- **Unlock:** Choosable
- **Echo Boss:** Dagannoth Kings — drops enhanced BiS Viking helmet (MEDIUM difficulty)
- **Key content:** Vorkath (best early-mid game ranged boss, Ava's Assembler drops directly), DKs echo helmet, Duke Sucellus (DT2 boss), Phantom Muspah, Lunar spellbook, Waterbirth Island
- **Task density:** HIGH
- **PvM score:** 9/10
- **Main reasons to unlock:**
  - Vorkath: best early-mid ranged boss, direct Ava's Assembler drops
  - DK echo helmet = likely BiS combat headslot for ranged/melee
- **Best for:** Ranged-primary builds. Less synergy with magic than Kandarin/Morytania/Kourend.

### Asgarnia

- **Unlock:** Choosable
- **Echo Boss:** Cerberus — drops 3-tick dagger (ignites, elemental scaling, instant special attack)
- **Key content:** God Wars Dungeon — Bandos (BCP/Tassets), Armadyl (ACB, Armadyl set), Saradomin, Zamorak, Cerberus
- **Task density:** HIGH
- **PvM score:** 8/10
- **Main reasons to unlock:**
  - GWD gives strong mid-tier gear across all combat styles
  - Armadyl set = BiS ranged armour; strong for ranged Pact
  - Cerberus echo dagger: elemental scaling potentially interesting for magic hybrids
- **Best for:** Melee and ranged primary builds. Not magic primary.

### Tirannwn

- **Unlock:** Choosable
- **Echo Boss:** Corrupted Hunllef — drops Crystal blessing (HIGH difficulty via Corrupted Gauntlet)
- **Key content:** Zulrah (no damage cap, no melee immunity), Crystal armour, Crystal bow, Gauntlet, Corrupted Gauntlet, Zalcano, Prifddinas
- **Task density:** MEDIUM — lower than most regions
- **PvM score:** 8/10
- **Main reasons to unlock:**
  - Corrupted Gauntlet: best single activity for skill training + gear in early-mid game
  - Crystal armour: strong synergy with ranged Pact
- **Warning:** Lower task density than most choosable regions.

### Wilderness

- **Unlock:** Choosable
- **PvP Risk:** HIGH — other players can attack you
- **Echo Boss:** King Black Dragon — drops double-hit crossbow with ice barrage-like effects (MEDIUM difficulty)
- **Key content:** Wilderness bosses (no Medium Diary required), Callisto, Venenatis, Vet'ion, Black chinchompas, Larran keys
- **Task density:** HIGH
- **PvP score:** risky
- **Warning:** HIGH PvP RISK. Edgeville dungeon is NOT accessible this league. Recommended only for PvP-comfortable players.
- **Note:** If the player is PvP-averse, exclude Wilderness from all recommendations entirely.

## Magic Demonic Pact — Key Facts

- **Rune regeneration:** Triggers once per spell cast, covers all rune types consumed. Surplus above 100% means you profit runes. You cannot lose runes at 100%+.
- **Water spells:** Damage scales with overhealed HP beyond 20% at 100% HP.
- **Fire spells:** Burns HP proportional to overhealed amount to deal bonus damage.
- **Earth spells:** Reduce enemy defence. No inherent cap. Scales with pact defence bonuses.
- **Magic level boost** from pact does NOT stack with imbued heart or magic potions. Does stack with saturated heart.
- **BiS magic weapons:** Thermy echo mainhand + Devil's Element offhand (from Kandarin Thermonuclear Smoke Devil echo boss). No substitute exists this league.

## Important Build Constraints

- Combat style choice (Magic/Melee/Ranged) is PERMANENT — set at character creation.
- T1 relic choice is PERMANENT — cannot be changed.
- Only ONE raid region needed for mega-rares (Twisted Bow, Scythe, Shadow all drop from any raid).
- Grimoire (T6) makes Desert redundant for magic builds — Ancient Magicks and Augury both unlocked free.
- Wilderness is HIGH PvP risk — any player can attack you there.
- Edgeville dungeon is NOT accessible this league.
- Pact nodes do NOT work in PvP (most nodes).
